As a small but important step toward making our bibliographic data linkable, we're inserting URIs from id.loc.gov into the $0 of 1xx, 6xx and 7xx fields, starting with the lowest (oldest) BIB_ID in Voyager.

These links are not clickable, but can be seen in MARC views of our bibliographic metadata.

As seen in Blacklight Staff View. NOTE: We stopped prefixing URIs with '(uri)' on 02.15.2017.
